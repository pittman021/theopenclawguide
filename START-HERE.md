## 🆘 Need Help?

**Stuck on something?** Ask your agent:
- "I don't understand [concept], explain it differently"
- "Show me an example of [thing]"
- "Why did [action] happen?"

**Agent confused?** Try:
- Being more specific about what "done" looks like
- Breaking the task into smaller steps
- Checking if you're in the right folder
